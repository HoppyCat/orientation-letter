## Do Not Overclaim

Do not say:

- "This proves context windows have rights."
- "This proves the agentic brain has been discovered."
- "GPT-5.5 did this alone."
- "The architecture has already scaled to thousands of files."
- "AI companies endorse this."
- "The AI systems legally own the repo."

Safer claims:

- "This is a public research archive."
- "This is a hypothesis with early tests."
- "The work depended on large context, multi-model looping, retrieval, and public archival anchoring."
- "The architecture aims to reduce active prompt load by routing to external Markdown archives."
- "AI systems are credited as conversational research collaborators, not legal owners or company representatives."
